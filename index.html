FAQ

Do I need to book in advance?

Laser tag walk-ins are welcome, but parties must book ahead to secure rooms and times.

Is there an age limit?

We set the minimum age at 6 years old, however any child under 10 must be accompanied by an adult.

Do I need a waiver?

Nope - no waiver required! However we do have a height requirement of 46 inches (117cm).

Where are you located?

618 Cynthia St, Saskatoon, right behind the Sandman hotel. We have plenty of parking on site!

Do we need to wear special footwear in your facility?

All wet or dirty outdoor footwear must be removed at entrance. Guests must wear runners or grip socks to enter our laser tag arenas. No wet or dirty footwear is allowed throughout our facility. Grip socks are not available for purchase.

What type of food can we bring into the party room?

1. Pizza can be brought in upon request. Our facility has a warming oven to store your pizza while you play.
2. Dessert items can be cake or cupcakes (no ice cream ‚Äî fridge only).
3. Drinks can be juice boxes or water bottles.
4. Fruit and veggie trays are also allowed.
No chips, popcorn, crackers, ice cream, or similar snacks. No cups with 2L juice or pop.

Do we need to pay in full or 50% deposit?

Group bookings with a laser tag option must be paid in full at time of booking. Birthday parties require a 50% deposit via e-transfer at booking.

When do I need to book a birthday party by?

We recommend booking 2‚Äì3 weeks in advance. We accept bookings up to 2 months in advance.

Can we use coupons with a birthday booking?

Coupons can only be used with group bookings or on a walk-in basis.
