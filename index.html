FAQ & Policies

How do I book?

Email us with your full name, phone number, and request. We’ll confirm availability.

Do I pay a deposit?

Birthdays: 50% e-transfer deposit. Groups: pay in full at booking.

What should we bring?

Clean, dry indoor runners. Water bottles. Cake and pizza for party rooms.
