FAQ & Policies

How do I book?

Email prairiejurassic17@shaw.ca with your full name and phone number. We’ll confirm availability and next steps.

Do I pay a deposit?

Birthday parties: 50% e-transfer deposit at time of booking. Group bookings with laser tag: pay in full at time of booking.

What should we bring?

Clean, dry indoor runners for all players. A spill-proof water bottle is recommended. Cake and pizza are welcome for party room bookings.

Are games private?

Games run on a schedule and are generally open to the public with up to ~30 players per game.
